      • Magic Cake

      Magic Cake is one of the tastiest cakes in the world has never known. Few have tried it and lived to tell about it, and there are their stories.

      Ingredients

    • 2 eggs
    • 1 pound of sugar
    • 3 sticks of butter

      • Directions

    • Mix eggs,sugar, and butter in a alrge bowl
    • Spread into a non-stick dish
    • Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour
    • Let sit at room temperature for 20 minutes
    • Eat and enjoy!
